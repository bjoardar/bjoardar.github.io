About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at University of Houston since 2022. Previously, I was a Postdoc (NSF CI-Fellow) at Duke University with Dr. Krishnendu Chakrabarty. I did my PhD from Washington State University where I was advised by Dr. Partha Pratim Pande and Dr. Janardhan Rao Doppa. My undergraduate is from Jadavpur University, India. Apart from research, I enjoy reading, going out on hikes and watching football (soccer). You can find my CV here.

My research interests include:

  • Manycore systems
  • In-memory computing
  • Emerging technologies
  • Accelerating Deep Learning
  • Reliable and Secure Machine Learning
