Sanmitra Banerjee received the B. Tech. degree from the Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur, in 2018. He is currently pursuing Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Duke University, Durham, NC. He was an intern with Texas Instruments India, Intel, and NVIDIA. His current research interests include fault modeling and optimization methods for post-Moore AI accelerators based on carbon nanotube FETs and silicon-photonic integrated circuits.
