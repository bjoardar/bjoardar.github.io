Harrison Hao-Yu Ku received the B.S. degree in computer science at National Tsing Hua University, Hsinchu, Taiwan, in 2020, and is currently pursuing the M.S. degree in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Duke University, Durham, NC, USA. His research interests include microfluidic biochips, reinforcement learning and Out-of-Distribution Detection. His recent project attempts to enhance throughput and reliability of bioassay operations carried out autonomously upon PCB-based digital microfluidic biochip via reinforcement learning.
