Sohini is a PhD student in the Dept. Of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Duke University. She received the B. Tech degree from the Indian Institute of Engineering Science and Technology (IIEST), Shibpur, India in 2019 in the Dept. Of Information Technology. Prior to joining Duke, she was a Technology Consultant at PwC, India (2019-21). Her research focuses on microfluidic biochips, machine learning and hardware security.
