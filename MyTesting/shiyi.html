Shiyi Jiang received a B.A. degree (magna cum laude) from Boston University, Boston, MA, in 2018. She received the M.Sc. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering with Data Analytics & Machine Learning concentration from Duke University, Durham, NC, in 2020, where she is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree. Her current research focuses on techniques to preserve privacy, enhance, and personalize healthcare data analysis in the IoT system.
