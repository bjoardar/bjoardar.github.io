Arjun Chaudhuri received the B.Tech. degree from the Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur, India, in 2017. He is currently pursuing Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Duke University, USA. He was an intern with Global Foundries Inc., Alibaba Computing Technology Lab., and NVIDIA. His current research interests include design-for-testability and fault tolerance of machine learning hardware, monolithic 3D integrated circuits, and carbon-nanotube FETs. He likes playing lawn tennis and going on road trips to the mountains.
