Aritra Ray completed his Bachelor of Technology in Information Technology from University of Calcutta (2017-21), India. He is interested in Applied Machine Learning research.

He joined Electrical and Computer Engineering Department, Duke University as a PhD student in Prof. Krishnendu Chakrabarty's research group in Fall 2021. At Duke, he harnesses Machine Learning for Uncertainty Quantification for medical image segmentation algorithms in collaboration with Duke Radiology, Duke University School of Medicine. In another collaboration with Duke University's School of Nursing, he addresses uncontrolled diabetes and hypertension through Machine Learning. He assists undergraduates in the lab on other projects like enlarging datasets to address scarcity of labelled data in health domain through Generative Adversarial Networks.

Hobbies include outdoor sports and reading books.
