Jayeeta received her B.E. degree in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering from Jadavpur University, India, in 2020. While an undergraduate, she did her summer internship at Duke University, USA. Jayeeta did a summer internship at Mentor Graphics in 2021, where she contributed to the software development domain. In Fall 2020, she joined the ECE department at Duke University to pursue her Ph.D. degree under the guidance of Prof. Krishnendu Chakrabarty. Her current research interests include hardware security and designing architecture and algorithms for securing analog integrated circuits.
